THE GREATER CHALLENGE

Written by The Martine Family

Alike in truths that we share

To learn is to be alive

What joy becoming aware

Of a school where excellence thrives

And so choosing and chosen we go

To find and to explore

The greater challenge of all that’s in store

No laboratory so fine

As testing ground we find here

No union of heart and mind

As friendships formed in these years

As we’re into worlds yet unknown

By vision clear and bright

The greater challenge remains in our sight

As long as ever we live

Whatever mountains we climb

Whatever we have to give

Was honed within that brief time

So may always our gratitude shine

In what we do and say

The greater challenge is ours every day.

Hear the Alma Mater sung.
